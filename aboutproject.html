About Project For Everyone

Project For Everyone (PFE) is a career development initiative designed to equip individuals with the tools, experience, and confidence they need to break into the workforce—no matter their background. At the heart of our mission is accessibility: we believe everyone deserves a chance to prepare for real-world roles in a supportive, inclusive, and practical environment.

Realistic Job Simulations

We provide immersive job simulations that replicate real workplace challenges across various entry-level roles—ranging from business analysis and customer support to digital marketing, data, and tech. These simulations are structured to reflect day-to-day responsibilities, allowing users to gain hands-on experience, build confidence, and demonstrate their skills.

Our in-house simulations are designed by career professionals and educators to mirror real tasks like:

  • Drafting emails or reports,
  • Prioritizing tasks under deadlines,
  • Making decisions based on real-time data, and
  • Collaborating with (fictional) team members to solve problems.

Partnered Scenarios with Employers

Beyond our internally developed experiences, Project For Everyone collaborates with clients and employers to create custom, branded job scenarios. These simulations reflect the exact tasks and expectations a candidate might face if they joined that company, giving users a unique preview of company culture and work style.

For employers, this is a powerful way to:

  • Educate prospective candidates,
  • Reduce early-stage hiring friction, and
  • Attract individuals with a demonstrated interest and relevant skills.

For users, it's a chance to experience what a role might truly feel like before applying.

About Short-course: Data Science with General Assembly (UK)

General Assembly's Data Science short course provides a thorough understanding of data analysis, machine learning, and statistical modeling. Students learn how to clean, visualize, and interpret complex datasets using tools like Python, SQL, and data visualization software. The course covers predictive modeling, data wrangling, and advanced analytics techniques, preparing participants to tackle real-world data challenges. With hands-on projects and industry-focused case studies, students gain the practical skills needed to make data-driven decisions and drive business insights in various sectors.
